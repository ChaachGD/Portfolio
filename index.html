Musictober


I tried to make a short song every day during October 2019, most of them were made under 3 hours.

Gambler's Justice

Gambler's Justice is a Visual Novel, Puzzle, Point and Click game about uncoververing the cheating methods of a casino. Visualy inspired by 90's Anime (Lupin III and Dominion Tank Police) with a smooth jazz soundtrack. It was made for the Community Gamejam 2019.

Dangers Afloat


Dangers Afloat is a party game full of mischief and mistrust where the crew must cooperate to find the lost treasure and the Traitor must take it to himself. It is still in development and will be released on 2020.

Entropy Scramble

Entropy Scramble is a 2D puzzle-platformer where you must modify and combine the game mechanics to overcome the challenges throughout the level.

Norato

Norato was my second university project, it is a 2D action game where you play as a man who is cursed and transforms into a snake during the day, you must defend a river from evil fishes and help people that fall on the water.

Dreams

Dreams is my first music EP, it has a chiptune style, featuring 3 tracks. Its production is currently on hold.
